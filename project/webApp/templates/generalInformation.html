Diversion on Routes 4, 7, 45a, 46a, 59, 63 and 75

Published on Thursday, July 27, 2017

We wish to advise customers that due to the DLR Bay 10k Road Race, Routes 4, 7, 45a, 46a, 59, 63 and 75 will have the below diversions in place from 10.00 to 13.00 on Monday 7 August.

Route 4 From City Centre

Buses are operating to and from the old terminus at Stradbrook/ Abbey Road.

Route 7/a From City Centre

At Temple Hill buses will divert right via Stradbrook Road towards Abbey Road Roundabout, Bakers Corner, Rochestown Avenue, Sallynoggin Road, Pearse Street and back on to normal route. Route 7/a From Brides Glen Luas Stop Normal route to Sallynoggin Church, Pearse Street, then via Sallynoggin Road, Rochestown Avenue, Bakers Corner, Abbey Road, Stradbrook Road, Temple Hill and back on to normal route.

Route 45a Towards Dun Laoghaire

Normal route to Rochestown Avenue, Bakers Corner, Abbey Road and terminate at last stop before roundabout. Route 45a Towards Ballywaltrim From roundabout at Abbey Road via Bakers Corner, Rochestown Avenue and back on to normal route.

Routes 46a, 63 and 75 Towards Dun Laoghaire

Normal route to Bakers Corner, left to Abbey Road and terminate at last stop before Abbey Road Roundabout. Routes 46a, 63 and 75 From Dun Laoghaire From roundabout at Abbey Road via Bakers Corner, Kill Avenue and back on to normal route.

Route 59 to and from Mackintosh Park

From roundabout at Abbey Road via Bakers Corner, Kill Avenue and back on to normal route.
