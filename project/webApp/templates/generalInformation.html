Bus Átha Cliath-Dublin Bus, a designated activity company, limited by shares, registered in Ireland at 59 Upper O’Connell St, Dublin 1. No 119569 was founded in 1987 and is a wholly owned subsidiary of the state-owned Córas Iompar Eireann Group. They operate the Public Service Obligation network in the Greater Dublin Area under a contract of services with the National Transport Authority. Our network covers a region from Newcastle in County Wicklow to the south, Balbriggan in north County Dublin and Maynooth in County Kildare to the west.

In 2015 they carried 122 million customers on their extensive range of services - Cross City, Radial, Orbital, DART Feeder, Airlink, express limited stop commuter services, Nitelink, and Sightseeing tours.

Dublin Bus employs 3,400 people from 68 different countries. Their 2,500 drivers operate a fleet of 950 buses which are all low-floor wheelchair accessible and fully Wi-Fi enabled.
